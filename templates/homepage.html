Our Story


We are a group passionate about finding good food and fostering friendship. While on our journey of exploring where are the best spots for food, we find that we can't find a perfect food recommendation platform that allows us to easily compile a list with friends. When we want to share food with friends, often we have to screenshot or send the url, and then compile the spots into a list on a separate doc.

Food sharing experience does not have to complicated and our platfrom aims to simplify its process while building that relationship with friends so that we can spend more time "NomNom"ing together





How It Works

1. Users will create an account such that the website can save all their information and personalize their webpage
2. Then they can explore the map tab on the website to search and find food locations.
3. When they found a location that they want to visit in the future, they can add the location to their list of restaurants
4. They can also add friends to the list such that they can collaborate on creating a list tailored to food their journey
